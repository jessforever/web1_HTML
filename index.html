Simply Bake!

Baking box is a kit which is composed of delicious recipies and lots of ingredients which will make you convenient to cook what you want

To bake things we need an oven, lots of ingredients that we won't use in daily life, tools that is quiet expensive to buy but don't use often.
These are the things that makes baking hard. So we decided to provide tools and ingredietns that are needed to bake somthing only once.
You don't need to worry about the space to put all the baking stuff and refrigerator to keep the ingredients fresh.
